TIPS & TRICKS

1. Remember the full phrase before starting to type

2. Remember the phrases which appear since they may be repeated

3. The game doesn't immediately stop when the time hits zero

4. You can temporarily stop the game by pressing instructions

5. Play more rounds and you definitely will get better at the game
